About Us

Mission Oaks Web Designs

Giving Small Businesses The Big Business Advantage

My name is Jeremy, the proud owner and sole developer of Mission Oaks Web Designs. My passion for programming was ignited by a desire for a flexible career that I could pursue from home. With this vision in mind, I dedicated two years to teaching myself the art of coding websites from scratch. Armed with newfound expertise, I took the initiative to offer my assistance to small businesses.

What I soon discovered was that many small businesses faced financial constraints, making it challenging for them to invest significant sums in a new website. Even those who managed to do so often ended up with poorly designed websites that appeared outdated and unappealing. This presented a frustrating dilemma - either they couldn't afford a quality website, or if they could, they struggled to find a reliable partner to create something exceptional. In response to this predicament, I adapted my business model to cater to their specific needs. That's how I came up with my $0 down and $150 a month model, providing a more manageable solution for small businesses without straining their budgets.
